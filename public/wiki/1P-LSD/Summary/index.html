1P-LSD/Summary
From PsychonautWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Main article: 1P-LSD

Psychoactive class: Psychedelic

Chemical class: Lysergamide

Oral

  • Thresh Common Heavy
    20 - 25 - 50 - 150 - 300 µg
    Light Strong
    • Total: 8 - 12 hours
    • Onset: 45 - 90 minutes
    • Peak: 2 - 4 hours
    • Offset: 2 - 4 hours

Harm potential

    • Not habit-forming
    • Toxic dose is unknown

Tolerance

    • Full tolerance is reached almost immediately after ingestion
    • Decreases to half after 3 days
    • Returns to baseline after 7 days
    • Cross-tolerance with all psychedelics

Physical effects

    • Pupil dilation
    • Spontaneous tactile sensations
    • Bodily control enhancement
    • Stimulation
    • Tactile enhancement
    • Increased heart rate
    • Muscle spasms
    • Nausea

Visual effects

    • Acuity enhancement
    • Colour enhancement

    • Colour shifting
    • Depth perception distortions
    • Drifting

    • Symmetrical texture repetition

    • Geometry: Eventually leads to level 8A geometry
    • Autonomous entities
    • Internal hallucinations
    • Perspective alterations
    • Scenarios and plots
    • Settings, sceneries, and landscapes
    • Transformations

Cognitive effects
