Mailing List (V2)

To facilitate multiple means of collaboration, the community has launched the PsychonautWiki mailing list.

This list should provide a forum for more formal and long-winded discussion than our IRC channel.

The mailing list facilitates relaxed and general discussion about all things concerning PsychonautWiki itself, psychonautics, and psychoactive drugs. The topic can be academic or non-academic.

Subscribe - Archives

Rules

  • Like the wiki, keep the conversation scientific and skeptical; this is no place for pseudoscience or mysticism.
  • During academic discussion, always quote empirical evidence for any theories you might have. During non-academic discussion, be polite and reasonable.
  • Do not discuss online sources of psychoactive goods.
  • Use common sense.

Please direct questions to Aleks (alekstk [at] riseup.net).
