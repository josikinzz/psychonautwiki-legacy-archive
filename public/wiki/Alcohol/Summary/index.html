Alcohol/Summary
From PsychonautWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Main article: Alcohol

Psychoactive class: Depressant

Chemical class: Alcohol

Harm potential

    • Extremely addictive with a high potential for abuse
    • Death from ethanol consumption is possible when blood alcohol levels reach 0.4%

Tolerance

    • Full tolerance is reached develops with prolonged and repeated use
    • Decreases to half after 3 - 7 days
    • Returns to baseline after 1 - 2 weeks
    • Cross-tolerance with all GABAgenic depressants

Physical effects

    • Muscle relaxation
    • Physical euphoria
    • Appetite enhancement
    • Motor control loss
    • Pain relief
    • Respiratory depression
    • Sedation
    • Tactile suppression
    • Dehydration
    • Difficulty urinating
    • Dizziness
    • Frequent urination
    • Headaches
    • Increased blood pressure
    • Nausea
    • Temporary erectile dysfunction

Visual effects

    • Acuity suppression
    • Double vision
    • Pattern recognition suppression

Cognitive effects

    • Cognitive euphoria
    • Compulsive redosing
    • Delusions
    • Depression
    • Ego inflation
    • Empathy, love, and sociability enhancement
    • Amnesia
    • Analysis suppression
    • Anxiety suppression
    • Creativity suppression
    • Disinhibition
    • Information processing suppression
    • Language suppression
    • Thought deceleration
    • Thought disorganization

Auditory effects

    • Auditory suppression
