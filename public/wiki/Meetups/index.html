Meetups
From PsychonautWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

As the PsychonautWiki community has grown substantially in the last two years, many of us have met in person, and others have expressed the desire to do so. We encourage the community to organise their own parties, but this page will be used to facilitate official events.

Although there are no solid plans as of yet, we must begin by gauging how many people would be interested, and where they are located. Please add your name to the relevant list if you are interested in participating in a gathering some time in 2016.
