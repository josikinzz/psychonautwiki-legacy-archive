Kratom/Summary
From PsychonautWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Main article: Kratom

Psychoactive class: Opioid

Chemical class: Indole alkaloid

Oral

  • Thresh Common Heavy
    1 - 2 - 3 - 5 - 8 g
    Light Strong
    • Total: 2 - 5 hours
    • Onset: 15 - 45 minutes
    • Peak: 1 - 2 hours
    • Offset: 3 - 6 hours

Harm potential

    • Moderately addictive with a high potential for abuse
    • Low toxicity

Tolerance

    • Full tolerance is reached develops with prolonged and repeated use
    • Decreases to half after 3 - 7 days
    • Returns to baseline after 1 - 2 weeks
    • Cross-tolerance with all other opioids

Physical effects

    • Physical euphoria
    • Pupil constriction
    • Stimulation
    • Cough suppression
    • Pain relief
    • Constipation
    • Difficulty urinating
    • Itchiness
    • Nausea

Visual effects

    • Acuity suppression
    • Double vision
    • Visual disconnection
    • Internal hallucinations

Cognitive effects

    • Cognitive euphoria
    • Motivation enhancement
    • Thought acceleration
    • Anxiety suppression
    • Decreased libido
