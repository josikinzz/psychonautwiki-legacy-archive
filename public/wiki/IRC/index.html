Rules

  • The codes of conduct for this IRC are simple.
    • Do not discuss online sources of illegal psychoactive goods. (severe)
    • Do not discuss drug dealing. (moderate)
    • Be polite, chilled out and reasonable. (mild)
    • Do not be a source of overly bad vibes. (moderate)
    • Do not beg for money. (mild)
    • Do not facilitate or encourage destructive behavior. (moderate)
    • Do not spam. This includes clogging up the channel with constant chatter and not allowing others to get a word in. If you are repeatedly having overly extended discussions with one other individual then please consider taking the conversation to private messages. (moderate)
  • Rule violations rated as severe will result in a permanent ban. Those listed as moderate will result in a temporary ban of 1 - 7 days. Violations of rules listed as mild will result in being kicked. Users who violate these rules will be verbally warned by an administrator who will explain why the punishment is occurring.

    If you wish to dispute a ban, you may contact us via psychonautwiki@gmail.com.

Bots

gonzobot - A fork of Cloudbot, which responds to commands prefixed with . or @.

rAnnouncer - Announces new content posted to r/Replications.

imagebot - Use !image <subreddit> to request a random image from a specified sub-reddit.

memebot - Use !memes to list, and !meme [m:memename] <toptext>;<bottomtext> to create memes.

MineBot - Relays messages between Snoonet's IRC server and our MineCraft server

Statistics - Records statistics for the channel.

weeedbot - Use !comic to produce a comic strip from the last few lines.
