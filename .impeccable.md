## Design Context

### Users

People browsing the preserved PsychonautWiki archive on phones as well as desktop computers. Their primary jobs are reading long reference articles, inspecting substance infoboxes and data tables, searching the archive, and moving between related pages. Mobile use is touch-first and may happen while reading for an extended period; the historical desktop presentation remains important to the archive.

### Brand Personality

Archival, eccentric, and information-dense. The mobile interface should feel like the genuine 2016 PsychonautWiki Vector skin adapted by its original maintainers, not like a contemporary redesign placed over it.

### Aesthetic Direction

Preserve the existing typography, colors, borders, imagery, and compact wiki character. Below the conventional 768px mobile boundary, reflow the site into a readable single column and place the original navigation inside a faithful 2016-style drawer. At 768px and wider, the rendered desktop layout must remain unchanged.

### Design Principles

1. Preserve the archive: adapt existing markup and navigation instead of replacing or restyling the historical desktop skin.
2. Make reading primary: body copy, headings, images, infoboxes, and tables must fit a phone without page-level horizontal scrolling.
3. Keep every useful route: navigation and search move into the drawer rather than disappearing on mobile.
4. Design for touch and keyboards: controls use at least 44px targets, visible focus states, Escape-to-close behavior, and accurate ARIA state.
5. Keep the retrofit reversible and repeatable: one shared compatibility layer is injected by the archive post-processing pipeline.
